Project Title: Video Creation for Social Media with Cap Cut

This project focuses on creating engaging and visually appealing videos tailored for social media platforms. The goal was to capture attention quickly and deliver content in a dynamic, user-friendly format. I used CapCut as the primary editing tool to enhance the video with visual effects, transitions, and animations, bringing the content to life. These creative elements were added to ensure the video was not only informative but also entertaining—avoiding a plain or static feel. The result is a polished, high-energy video designed to engage viewers and encourage interaction.
